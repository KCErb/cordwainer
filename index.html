Cordwainer is my notes on how Shoes works. They are written in the style of a tutorial because I learn best when I teach. The goal of these notes/ tutorials is to learn how Shoes works from heel to toe, frontend to backends, do to end. Please feel free to comment on inaccuracies, typos or anything else, I'd love to improve these!
